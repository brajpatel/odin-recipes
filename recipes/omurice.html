Omurice

Description

Once practiced, omurice becomes a relatively simple dish to make.

It's the perfect to make for breakfast and lunch and makes for a nice and filling meal.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups cooked white rice
  • 2 mushrooms
  • ½ onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • Salt
  • Vegetable or Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Chop up the mushrooms, onions and garlic
  2. Heat up some oil in a medium/small pan. Once the oil is hot, add the vegetables with the 1 tablespoon of ketchup and soy sauce.
  3. When the onions have become clear, add the cooked rice and the rest of the ketchup and soy sauce.
  4. Mix everything together thoroughly. Once the rice is hot, put it all in a bowl and set aside.
  5. Put a small amount of oil in the pan and pour the beaten eggs in.
  6. Lift the pan and tilt it around so the egg covers all areas of the pan.
  7. Let the egg cook while occasionally lifting it up from the sides so it doesn't stick.
  8. Once the egg has cooked all the way through, place it on to your plate and fold it over.
  9. Add the rice to your plate, place the folded egg on top and top with some ketchup.
