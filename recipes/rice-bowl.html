Rice Bowl

Description

Rice is a very simple but important recipe. It is component of a huge variety of dishes across different cultures.

Being able to cook rice well will allow you to create many new dishes in the future. Ready to learn how? Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Short grain white rice
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice - put the desired amount of rice in a bowl and fill with some water. Stir the rice with your hands, the water will likely become murky. Drain the water and repeat until the water becomes mostly clear.
  2. Place the rice in the pot you'll be cooking it in and add water. A good rule of thumb is to add twice as much water as there is rice (E.g. for 1 cup of rice, add 2 cups water).
  3. Allow the rice to soak for approximately 45 minutes to an hour.
  4. To start cooking the rice, set the heat to high and bring the water to the boil.
  5. Once the water has come to a boil, turn the heat down to medium and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  6. Turn the heat down to low and leave to cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Then turn the heat off and let it sit for a final 5 minutes.
  8. Lastly, strain the rice, serve and enjoy!
